Why I chose these website ideas

    2. Through this website I hope to advertize services I could provide, such as Art Therapy, Murals, Commissions, Catalogue, Portfolio, and an about me section (the least fun)...

    4. I was just trying to think of a website that I'd love to just explore, and such would be an archive of many creative coders and their works...
