Hello world! This is an art collective, currently based solely in Missoula, MT. DHCs goal is to hopefully engageand amass a community that can be used to host collaborations, art events, find artists and etc... We are constantly evolving our approach and are welcome to as many ideas as any of you are willing to offer! What brought upon the creation of this movement was our collective realization of the powers within the creation, viewing and engagement in art. It's place within society is both therapuetic and simultaneously transcendental. It engages our brain in seemingly nootropic ways. Here are just a few...

  • Stimulates the brains ability to form and reorganize synaptic connections
  • Improves brain plasticity
  • Stimulates both hemispheres of the brain (promoting reduction of stress)

We are a story... About... A Museum of Consciousness...
